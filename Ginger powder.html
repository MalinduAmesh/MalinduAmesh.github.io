Ginger powder

Product Description

Dry ginger powder is widely used in the kitchen for cooking & preparation purpose to add flour & aroma. The powder is extracted from the ginger root which is dried.

It is a fine off-white or slightly brownich powder with a strong aroma. It has a long shelf life near to 1 year.
